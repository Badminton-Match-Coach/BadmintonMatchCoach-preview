Privacy Policy

Badminton Match Coach respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is stored, and how it is used.

Data collected

  • Match event details entered by users, including rally outcomes, shot types, and scoring events.
  • Match metadata such as player names, competition details, and session timestamps.
  • Local session state used to recover match progress after reloads or app restarts.
  • Optional .BmMatch files opened or exported by the user.

How data is used

Match data is used only to provide analysis, scoring, undo/redo history, and report generation. Data remains local unless you choose to export a .BmMatch file or share a report.

Storage and offline use

The app stores data locally in your browser or device storage so it can resume a match session and preserve state. It is designed to work offline as a Progressive Web App once installed.

Permissions

No location, camera, or personal account permissions are required. The app only requests storage access when saving or opening a match file.

Contact
