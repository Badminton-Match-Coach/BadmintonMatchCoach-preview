Badminton Match Coach

Turn your coach, teammate, or friend into a digital match analyst! Record your badminton match live and get instant insight into why you win or lose points. The analysis is ready as soon as the match ends—or even during the break between games. How it works Step 1: Hand over your device Give your phone or Android tablet to your coach, teammate, or a friend. They operate the app from the bench while you focus on playing. Step 2: Record the match Your assistant taps in how each rally ends by recording: The serv, where the final shot was hit from, where it landed, the outcome of the rally, and the type of shot used. Optionally, they can also record every shot during the rally for even more detailed analysis. Step 3: Get instant analysis As soon as the match is over—or during a break between games—you'll have a complete analysis showing exactly how you won and lost your points. Discover your patterns Your mistakes Do you make more unforced errors at the beginning or end of games? Which shot types cause you the most problems? Should you play with larger margins on certain shots? Opponent's mistakes Does your opponent make more mistakes from specific court positions? Do they struggle in longer rallies? Which areas should you target more often? Winning patterns Is your smash, net play, drop shot, or another shot winning you the most points today? Tactical insights Where does your opponent win most of their points? Can you defend those areas better or avoid giving them those opportunities? Where do you win your points? Can you create more of those situations? Match analysis At any time during the match, between games, or after the match, you can view detailed statistics, including: Heat maps Shot type statistics Rally length statistics Every rally played during the match You can also open any rally to see exactly how it unfolded, shot by shot. Share your analysis Share your match analysis easily using your device's Share function: BmMatch file (the recipient also needs the app), or HTML file that can be opened in any web browser
