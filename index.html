About the app

Record your badminton match live and receive analysis as soon as the match ends, or even during the break between games.

How it works

  1. Hand over your device
    Give your phone or Android tablet to your coach, teammate, or friend so they can operate the app while you focus on playing.
  2. Record the match
    Your assistant can log how each rally ends by recording the serve, where the final shot was hit from, where it landed, the rally outcome, and the shot type.
  3. Get instant analysis
    As soon as the match is over, or during a break between games, you will see a complete breakdown of how you won and lost points.

Your mistakes

Opponent's mistakes

Do they make more mistakes from specific court positions or in longer rallies?

Winning patterns and tactics

Is your smash, net play, drop shot, or another shot winning you the most points today? Where does your opponent win most of their points, and how can you respond better?

Match analysis

At any point during the match, between games, or afterward, you can view detailed statistics including:

  • Heat maps
  • Shot type statistics
  • Rally length statistics
  • Every rally played during the match

Detailed rally analysis lets you open any rally and see exactly how it unfolded shot by shot.

Share your analysis using your device's Share function, either as a BmMatch file or as an HTML file that can be opened in any web browser.
